# DeskPal
You'll need an Arduino Uno an I2C oled screen (0.96 inch) and a RC522 RFID reader for this.
1. Connect the parts as drawn in the photo below
   ![circuit.png](circuit.png)
2. Upload the .ino sketch to your arduino or other microcontroller.
    1. Dont forget to change the RFID codes to the RFID chips you have. It shows the codes of your RFID chips in the Serial Monitor when you scan them.
3. 3D print the enclosure or build your own.
4. Assemble!
